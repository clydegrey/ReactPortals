Special Recognition

Lee Memorial Hospital is a 355-bed acute care facility and is the flagship hospital for Lee Health. Lee Memorial Hospital and The Rehabilitation Hospital employ more than 1,400 individuals, have a medical staff of more than 1,000 and benefit from the generosity of more than 500 volunteers. Located within Lee Memorial Hospital is The Rehabilitation Hospital, a 60-bed comprehensive inpatient rehabilitation facility. It is the only licensed comprehensive inpatient rehabilitation facility in Lee County. Lee Memorial Hospital also has the only Level II Trauma Center between Miami and Sarasota.
